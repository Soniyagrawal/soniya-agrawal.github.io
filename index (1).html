About

I'm a second-year MPP candidate at UC Berkeley's Goldman School of Public Policy, building at the intersection of AI governance, tech accountability, and data-driven policy research.

Before pivoting to policy, I spent five years as a Senior Correspondent at ThePrint, India's largest digital news organization, publishing over 600 articles and winning the International Press Institute Award for Excellence in Journalism for frontline COVID-19 investigative reporting. I helped scale ThePrint's YouTube from 100K to over 2 million subscribers.

Today, my work spans lobbying analysis, regulatory compliance frameworks, and AI safety evaluations — translating complex technical realities into actionable governance insights. I bring international experience across India, Mexico, and the United States, and a deep commitment to ensuring technology serves the public interest.
